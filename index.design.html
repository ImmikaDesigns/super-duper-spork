Becoming a Web Developer

If someone had told me a few weeks ago that I would be able to make a website, I would not have believed them. Being of Generation X, 46 years old, this sort of technology was not around for most of my life.


I had dabbled in apps like Shopify and was totally confused by the jargon involved in creating a successful website store.


More recently, I found WordPress to be more user friendly, but was curious about the html mode. And what was meant by snippets and widgets?!


Well, eventually I found Mimo and now things are making sense, and... here I am, creating a real blog from scratch!
